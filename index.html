About Us

We are 5 students from The University of Ottawa and we have decided to solve an issue plaguing us and the rest of Canada for years,waiting in clinics. The average wait time for walkin clinics are [Time], there are better things you can do with that time, instead of waiting at a clinic, you can do some shopping, get food etc. We have designed an app that works with your clinic to put you in a virtual waiting room instead of a real one while displaying an accurate wait time so you can do what's important, it is called WaitAway.

Why Us

When looking online we found many waiting room apps but all of them had one main thing in common, they were for restaurants. This is what makes us different we have a great idea that clearly works, as seen by the saturation of the restaurant market, but we are going into a market with no other competition.
